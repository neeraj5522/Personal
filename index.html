Foodie

Some eat to survive and some survive to eat. And, no doubt, Neeraj just belongs to the latter group; an outright foodie. He just loves to eat. Belonging to Nepal, his favourite delicacy is Dal Bhaat (rice and lentil) with chicken curry. More so, he is on a mission to try out every cuisine there is on this planet.

Creative

Neeraj has no special talents, he is just passionately curious. He never shies away from any challenge put in front of him, loves to experiment with new things, and knows how to accept and give constructive criticism. The fact that doing things the old-fashioned way bores him to death is just an exemplification of his creativeness. He might not consider himself a creative person, but his peers sure do.

Traveller

Travelling is the calling of the soul.' The words to which Neeraj lives by. He has a thing for aesthetic and chill places, be it natural or manmade. Born in a naturally gifted nation Nepal, he has yet to witness its beauty in its full glory. With a hope to explore every mile of this beautiful little planet, Neeraj is on the move.

Analytics

Being a stock market geek, Neeraj has always been analytical of things. He has this tendency to extract data out of any situation or happening using tactics. He is dubious, he is detail-oriented and is curious all of which makes him a good analyst.

Photography

Neeraj doesn't shy away from trying his hand at something new. So basically a novice, Neeraj developed a fascination towards photography thanks to his love of travelling. It gets him worked up to capture a beautiful sight into a photograph every time he Reads one. Despite having yet to learn the sufficient skill, he's pretty excited about learning photography.
